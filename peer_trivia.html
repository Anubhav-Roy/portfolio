Peer Trivia

Allows random people to connect with each other and quiz them about a mutual interest through VoIP ( implemented through WebRTC). When people are connecting , the matchmaking happens in the backend by stashing the player's Id in Redis ( and clearing it when they disconnect ), and connecting when someone new joins.
